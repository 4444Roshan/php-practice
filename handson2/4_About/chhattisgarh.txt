Chhattisgarh 
is a heavily forested state in central India known for its temples and waterfalls. 
Near the capital Raipur, the town of Sirpur on the Mahanadi River is home to the red-brick Lakshmana (Laxman) Temple, 
decorated with carvings from Indian mythology. In the south, the city of Jagdalpur hosts the Sanjay Market on Sundays, a bartering place for local tribes. 
The huge Chitrakoot Falls lie to the northwest
